💖 Couple's Connection App Specification
Target Platforms: iOS & Android
Core Tech Stack: Expo (React Native), Firebase (Auth & Realtime DB, natively supported by Antigravity), Google Maps API.

Core Features & Functionality:

Real-time Location & Status: A shared map interface that displays live location, speed, duration of stay, and device battery levels.

Private Chat & "Miss You" Signals: Secure, 1-on-1 messaging that includes buttons to send full-screen, romantic visual effects and haptic "pings."

Shared Memory Stories: A timeline or story-map where users can log daily life, sweet moments, and past trips together.

Virtual Pets: A shared digital pet that couples feed and interact with directly in the app to keep engagement fun.

Anniversaries & Widgets: Shared countdowns for special dates, seamlessly integrated with iOS and Android home screen widgets.
